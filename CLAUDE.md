|---|---|

## Hard Constraints

- **Timeout:** 10 minutes max per task.
- **Max iterations:** 3 retries, then stop.
- **Design:** Airbnb-style (light, whitespace, shadows, cards)
- **No frameworks** — vanilla HTML/CSS/JS only
- **Git:** Always commit and push after changes
